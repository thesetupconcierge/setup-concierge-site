Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

1. Who We Are

Setup Concierge LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a business consulting and formation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit setupconcierge.com (the "Site") or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number submitted through our intake form or lead magnet signup.
  • Business Information: Business stage, service interests, and goals you share in our intake form.
  • Communications: Any messages you send to us via email or form submissions.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics: We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics service. It collects aggregate data (page views, referral sources, country) without using cookies, tracking individuals, or collecting personal data.
  • Server Logs: Our hosting provider may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and intake form submissions
  • To deliver requested resources (e.g., business launch guide)
  • To communicate about our services
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Formspree (form processing), our email provider, and hosting services — only as needed to operate the Site.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or government request.

5. Cookies

This Site does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. Plausible Analytics is entirely cookieless. No cookie consent banner is required.

6. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to provide our services and communicate with you. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at admin@setupconcierge.com.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption and secure form processing. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
